Actor Mathew Baker has died aged 51 following a short illness

Richard O. Gannon, M. • Dec 30, 1895

BREAKING NEWS: Actor Mathew Baker has died aged 51 following a short illness which he contracted due to work related injuries. A towering figure amongst the StarCity’s most esteemed citizens, Mathew had spent nearly all his adult life pursuing success in the entertainment industry.

Following his childhood heroics, he poured his heart and soul into raising hopes and dreams for those in need of a change of pace, believing that through his unique gift, he could one day become like his grandfather, Victor Hugo.

Through perseverance and the determination of his friends, however, he has failed to achieve this aim, failing despite countless efforts of those around him.

As such, in honour of his grandfather, he has left behind a legacy of crime, social disorder, and despair which no amount of policy or social program modification will ever be able to put back into check.
